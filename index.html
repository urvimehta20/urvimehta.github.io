Bio

I'm Urvi Mehta, a data science professional who is passionate about exploring the intersection of creativity, technology, and innovation. My journey began with an innate curiosity about the little things I encountered, which has since evolved into a mission to create cutting-edge solutions that empower individuals and organizations.

Currently, I thrive in crafting generative AI models and leveraging large language models (LLMs) to solve real-world challenges. My approach combines technical rigor with creative problem-solving. Apart from this, I enjoy engaging with like-minded individuals, brainstorming ideas, and simplifying complex concepts for diverse audiences. Communication and collaboration are at the heart of all my projects and presentations.
